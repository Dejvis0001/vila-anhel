Vila Anhel

The best vacation destination!

Welcome to Vila Anhel, your perfect home away from home in the beautiful coastal town of Ksamil, Albania. Just 600 meters from the stunning Ksamil Beach, our accommodation offers a blend of comfort, convenience, and tranquility.

Whether you're here for a family vacation, a romantic getaway, or a solo retreat, Vila Anhel provides everything you need for a memorable stay.
